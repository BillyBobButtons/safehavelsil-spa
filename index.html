Who We Are

A Safe Place to Call Home

Safe Haven SLC is an NDIS residential care provider dedicated to offering genuine, one-on-one supported living for people with disability. Our homes in The Peninsula are exactly that — real homes, filled with warmth, routine, and respect.

We believe that living well isn't about receiving care — it's about building a life that is meaningful, connected, and yours. Our team works alongside each resident to understand what matters most to them and to make it happen every single day.

  • NDIS provider and fully compliant
  • Individually tailored support plans
  • Consistent, trusted support workers
  • Transparent communication with families
  • Regular goal reviews and progress reporting

What We Offer

Supports Tailored to Your Life

Our supports span every part of daily life — from morning routines to community adventures — always guided by what each resident wants and needs.

Supported Daily Living

Assistance with personal care, meal preparation, household tasks, and everyday routines — delivered with dignity at the resident's own pace.

24/7 Overnight Support

Round-the-clock care ensures residents feel safe and supported at every hour, with awake overnight staff available when needed.

Community Access

Supported participation in social, recreational, and community activities — from local markets to beach walks and everything in between.

Health & Wellbeing

Coordination of medical appointments, medication management, and allied health supports — keeping residents healthy and thriving.

Behaviour Support

Positive behaviour support strategies developed in collaboration with specialists — focusing on quality of life and meaningful engagement.

Family & Carer Liaison

Regular updates, open-door communication, and inclusive planning with families, guardians, and support coordinators.

Where We Are

Life on the Central Coast

Our homes are nestled in The Peninsula — a peaceful, close-knit community at the southern tip of the NSW Central Coast. Surrounded by waterways, national parks, and the gentle pace of coastal life, it's a setting that naturally supports wellbeing.

Residents have easy access to the beach, local shops, cafés, and community events. The The Peninsula waterfront and Brisbane Water are right on the doorstep — perfect for morning walks, fishing, or simply sitting in the sun.

How We Work

Our Person-Centred Approach

Great care starts with listening. We take the time to understand each person's story, goals, and preferences — then build support around that.

01

Getting to Know You

Before anything else, we sit down with the resident, their family, and support coordinator to understand who they are — what they love, what they find challenging, and what a good life looks like to them.

02

Building Your Plan

Together we co-design a support plan that maps out daily routines, personal goals, and the specific types of support that will help the resident live as independently and fully as possible.

03

Matching the Right Team

We carefully match residents with support workers who share their interests and communicate in ways that work for them — consistency and genuine connection are non-negotiable.

04

Continuous Review

Life changes — and so should support. We hold regular check-ins with residents and families to review progress, celebrate wins, and adjust plans so they always reflect what matters now.

NDIS & Funding

Understanding Your NDIS Pathway

Navigating the NDIS can feel overwhelming. Our team is here to help make the pathway to supported living as clear and straightforward as possible.

Supported Independent Living (SIL)

Our services are funded under the Supported Independent Living category of NDIS plans. SIL funding covers the cost of supports in a shared or individual residential setting.

Talk to Your Support Coordinator

If you have a Support Coordinator or Local Area Coordinator (LAC), they can help explore whether a residential support option like ours is appropriate for your plan goals.

Contact Us for a Vacancy Chat

Reach out to our team directly to discuss current or upcoming vacancies, arrange an introductory visit, and ask any questions about how our homes operate.

Trial & Transition Support

Moving into a new home is a big step. We offer gradual transition support and trial stays to ensure the fit is right for the resident before any permanent arrangement begins.

Safe Haven SLC is an NDIS provider. We work with participants, families, and support coordinators to ensure all supports are delivered in accordance with the NDIS Practice Standards and Code of Conduct.
